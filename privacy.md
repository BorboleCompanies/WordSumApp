# Privacy Policy for WordSum

**Last Updated: February 26, 2026**

## Introduction

WordSum ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the WordSum mobile application (the "App") on iOS or Android.

By using WordSum, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### Information Collected Automatically

**Game Progress Data (Stored Locally)**
- Daily puzzle completion status
- Streak counters and statistics
- Performance metrics (time, attempts, win rates)
- Game preferences and settings

This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

**Subscription Information**
- Subscription status and purchase history
- Transaction records managed by Apple's App Store or Google Play

This information is collected and managed by Apple or Google through their respective app stores and is subject to their privacy policies. We only receive confirmation of your subscription status to enable app features.

### Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:
- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Photos or camera access
- Contacts or social connections
- Device identifiers beyond what's required for subscription management
- Usage analytics or tracking data
- Advertising identifiers

## How We Use Your Information

### Game Data
Your local game progress, statistics, and preferences are used solely to:
- Track your daily progress and maintain streaks
- Display performance statistics
- Provide a personalized gaming experience
- Save your game state across app sessions

This data never leaves your device.

### Subscription Data
Your subscription information is used to:
- Verify your active subscription status
- Enable access to daily puzzles and features
- Process subscription renewals through Apple's or Google's systems

## Data Storage and Security

**Local Storage**
All game data is stored locally on your device using standard platform storage mechanisms. This data:
- Remains on your device
- Is included in device backups (if enabled)
- Is deleted if you delete the app
- Is not accessible to us or third parties

**Cloud Backups**
If you have iCloud (iOS) or Google Backup (Android) enabled on your device, your game data may be included in your personal backups. This is managed entirely by Apple or Google and subject to their respective privacy practices.

## Third-Party Services

### Apple App Store and StoreKit (iOS)
WordSum uses Apple's App Store and StoreKit framework for subscription management on iOS. When you subscribe, Apple processes your payment and manages your subscription. We receive only subscription status confirmation.

Apple's collection and use of your data is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

### Google Play and Google Play Billing (Android)
WordSum uses Google Play and Google Play Billing for subscription management on Android. When you subscribe, Google processes your payment and manages your subscription. We receive only subscription status confirmation.

Google's collection and use of your data is governed by [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

### No Other Third Parties
WordSum does not integrate with any other third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

## Your Data Rights

### Access Your Data
All your game data is stored locally on your device and can be viewed within the app's statistics section.

### Delete Your Data
To delete your data:
1. Delete the WordSum app from your device
2. All local game data will be permanently removed

To delete backup data:
- **iOS**: Manage your iCloud backups through iOS Settings
- **Android**: Manage your backups through Google Settings

### Manage Subscriptions

**iOS:**
1. Open iOS Settings
2. Tap your Apple ID at the top
3. Tap "Subscriptions"
4. Select WordSum
5. Manage or cancel your subscription

**Android:**
1. Open the Google Play Store app
2. Tap your profile icon
3. Tap "Payments & subscriptions" then "Subscriptions"
4. Select WordSum
5. Manage or cancel your subscription

Subscription management is handled entirely through Apple or Google. We cannot access or modify your subscription.

## Children's Privacy

WordSum is rated 4+ (iOS) and Everyone (Android), and is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children or users of any age. All game data is stored locally on the device.

## Data Retention

**Game Data**: Retained locally on your device until you delete the app or clear the data.

**Subscription Data**: Managed by Apple or Google according to their data retention policies. We do not store subscription history on our servers.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Continued use of WordSum after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## International Users

WordSum is available worldwide. All data processing occurs locally on your device. Subscription processing is handled by Apple or Google according to your region's app store policies.
